Chicken

Chicken is one of the most commonly consumed meats worldwide. It is especially popular among fitness enthusiasts because it is a great source of protein.
High-protein foods can help you reach your health and fitness goals, such as building muscle, maintaining muscle and losing fat However, chicken comes in a variety of cuts, including breasts, thighs, wings and drumsticks. Beef
